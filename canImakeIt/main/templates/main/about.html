2.Finding Recipes

Kitchens & Ingredients

  • After you have logged in, select the 'Create Kitchen' button on the navigation bar on the left

  • Choose a name for your Kitchen and select 'Create New Kitchen'.

  • You will now be navigated to your 'Manage Kitchen' page. Here you may enter ONE INGREDIENT AT A TIME in the prompt box and then select 'Add Ingredient'. The Ingredient will pop up, and you may repeat the process with more ingredients. If you want to remove an Ingredient, select the tick box next to it's name and hit the 'Remove Selected' button

  • After all your Ingredients are entered, select a Recipe Site to search for Ingredients. This will take you to a new page where recipes found will appear. Click on any one of them and you will be navigated to the corresponding website where the recipe is located
